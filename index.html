Our story, began at Roanoke College.

As fate would have it, they had both been placed in Crawford hall and lived on the same floor. Jon and Jenny had been on different paths, Jenny pursuing a degree in phycology and Jon was pursing a degree in Economics. Jon had become a member the Sigma Chi fraternity, and Jenny a member of Phi Mu sorority. Jenny was President of the Panhellenic Council and Jon quickly grew a crush on the smart and sexy girl when he became the treasure of the Inter-fraternity council. The crush grew into love and they began to frequently see each other at parties and other events. After casually dating for months, they finally made if official their senior year. After graduating, Jon headed back to New Jersey and Jenny back to northern Virginia.

Then we moved, to Jackson Hole, Wyoming.

After spending most of the Summer traveling back and forth between New Jersey and Virginia to see each other. In the Fall Jon decided to head Jackson Hole to fulfill a dream of being a ski-bum. Shortly after, Jon was able to convince Jenny who had never been skiing or past the Mississippi to move out to Wyoming with him. Jon wanted Jenny to enjoy skiing as much as he did and spent countless hours teaching Jenny to ski that winter, and she grew to love it despite the challenging terrane. They fell deeply in love with each other and their new home that winter.

Got a dog, named Zeus.

After their first winter, Jon and Jenny decided that living in Jackson would not be complete with a furry companion. On their first trek back to the east coast, Jon and Jenny adopted their golden retriever Zeus in Virginia.What was supposed to be one winter in Jackson quickly became three winters and two summers.

Then we moved Again, this time to San Francisco, California.

After almost three years in Jackson, in 2012 Jon and Jenny moved to New Jersey in the pursuit of graduate school and “real-life” jobs. That Fall Jon accepted a job offer in San Francisco and the two found themselves packing up their belongings once more and trekking across the country for their next adventure in California. Jenny continued to presume a carrier in School Counseling enrolled in a Graduate Program. Even with their busy work and school schedules they are still able to take advantage of living in the Bay Area. Jon and Jenny still spend most of their winter in Tahoe Skiing and take advantage of living close to the beach and great hikes when its not foggy in the city.

He finally popped the question, and she said yes!

Since moving to San Francisco, Jon had started biking a lot. He completed a couple centuries and bikes to work when ever he can. Jon and Jenny both enjoyed biking in Jackson Hole, but she left her bike when they moved to California since it got trashed in Hurricane Sandy. Jon wanted to share his new passion with Jenny and purchased her a new bike. A couple months later while training for his next century Jon purchased himself a new bike and begged Jenny to go on the first ride with him. Jenny assumed Jon wanted her to tag along to take a picture of him with his new bike at the top of the mountain, little did Jenny know, Jon had a something special planed. Jon led her across the Golden Gate Bridge to the Marin Headlands towards Hawk Hill, a mountain accent which he claimed would be a quick 10 minutes climb. Many, many minutes after the first 10 minutes and several breaks later, they arrived at the top of the mountain. There, wearing spandex and covered in sweat Jon went down on one knee with a magnificent view of San Francisco in the background he popped the question! Excited to learn she wasn’t just there to be a photographer, she was ecstatic to say YES!
